Overview of DH-FaceVid-1K

FaceVid-1K contains over 200,000 video clips featuring more than 150,000 unique identities, with 80% representing Asian individuals.

Face Video Datasets Comparison

Compared with other datasets, FaceVid-1K has a larger data volume, competitive quality, and richer attribute annotations.

Statics
